[STEAM_TITLE:A Dance of Six Legs]
[STEAM_DESCRIPTION:A Dance of Six Legs aims to add in new insectoid races and civilizations to your worlds, populating it with more raiders, traders and tree-dwelling hippies! As of now, the mod adds in two new civilizations and one new race; The Termidors – termite-men who dwell in the savannah and the shrublands, fighting off their age-old rivals, the evil, bloodthirsty Antmen. Flimsy and diminutive, Termidors thrive in the world due to their adaptability and their high birth rates. Females lay eggs instead of giving live birth, and the termidors’ short lifespan of only a handful of years means that they’re ready to work for the colony from a very young age. Termidors have access to wooden weapons and armour, including bows and blowguns, allowing them to quickly and cheaply produce stop-gap armaments before metals can be exploited. Sites without metal are now slightly more viable! The Antmen are, currently, the same as the Termidors. They share the same base stats and capabilities of the Termidors, but are evil and non-playable. More is planned for the Antmen, and other races such as spiders and crabs. Special thanks to Beep’s Snailing Race, which served as a handy template for this mod! I highly recommend you download it and enable it, to add more invertebrates to your world.]
