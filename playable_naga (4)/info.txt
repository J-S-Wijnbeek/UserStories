This mod changes the animals available to Naga to increase the distinctness of their civ.

Their animals are: Saltwater Crocodiles, Black Mambas, Copperhead Snakes, Anacondas, Capybaras, Tapirs, Spider Monkeys, Alligator Snapping Turtles, and Giant Pythons.

Saltwater Crocodiles, Black Mambas, Anacondas, and Giant Pythons are trainable for war.

Alligator Snapping Turtles are your source of shell, for making Shellsteel.

Giant Pythons have their price increased, and lay only a single egg.

Saltwater Crocs are Wagon Pullers, and Tapirs are pack animals. Spider Monkey fur is usable for yarn.

Copperhead Snakes replace cats as vermin hunters that adopt owners. They are sped up a bit.

Nagas are now active in the summer (caravans).]
